Typography

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type (letters, numbers, symbols) to make written language legible, readable, and visually appealing, involving choices in font, size, spacing, and layout to create mood and effectively convey a message for both print and digital media. It's a fundamental aspect of graphic design that breathes life into text, transforming it from plain words into a powerful communication tool for branding, web design, and everyday materials.
